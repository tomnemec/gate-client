Safety Instructions
  • Please wear your identification badge so that it is visible at all times.
  • With the exception of the marked smoking area, smoking is prohibited in the whole plant. Photographing and filming is strictly forbidden within the complete premises.
  • Please do not touch any machines, tools, or materials.
  • It is forbidden to bring any weapons (including dummies, toys) into the company.
  • Entrance to all areas of the company without the attendance of an HMCZ employee is strictly forbidden.
  • Please be cautious of transport vehicles. The routes are for pedestrians as well as material transport. Please stay on the designated routes.
  • Observe the rules of the EDS protected space.
  • Production areas are monitored by a camera system. Do not leave your group. Stay close to an HMCZ employee and follow his/her instructions.
  • In case of driving in the company grounds, keep to the maximum allowed speed limit of 10 km/h, and do not park outside of reserved parking places.
  • Internal emergency number: 454. In case of an emergency, please call and follow an HMCZ employee.

By entering this area, you agree to follow the safety instructions and consent to the storage of provided data in the HMCZ database.
